About US

  • We are a small design company that loves old school games, we're trying to bring a twist back to the 20th century.
  • Tic tac toe is by no means an unfamiliar game we're trying to help couples and siblings settle arguments about their chores & have some fun while it's been accomplish.
  • Basic rules apply you have three "X" or "O" to match for a winner, we plan to implement more games in the future such as the legendary snake game!
